About the role

Morpheus Labs is seeking a talented Web3 Software Engineer Intern to join our blockchain development team in Singapore. This is an exciting opportunity to work at the forefront of blockchain technology, building decentralized applications and contributing to our blockchain-as-a-service platform.

As a Web3 Engineer Intern, you'll gain hands-on experience with blockchain technologies, smart contract development, and decentralized application architecture. You'll work closely with our development team to build and enhance blockchain solutions for enterprise clients.

What you'll do

  • Develop and test smart contracts on various blockchain platforms (Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon)
  • Build frontend interfaces for decentralized applications using React and Web3.js
  • Integrate blockchain wallets and transaction functionality
  • Collaborate with the product team to implement new blockchain features
  • Assist in documentation and technical writing for blockchain implementations
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to our technical knowledge base

About Morpheus Labs

Morpheus Labs is a blockchain-platform-as-a-service company that provides enterprises with a streamlined way to build and deploy blockchain applications. Our platform enables businesses to develop, test, and manage decentralized solutions without having to manage complex blockchain infrastructure.

We're a dynamic team of blockchain enthusiasts working to make blockchain technology more accessible to enterprises worldwide. Join us in our mission to accelerate blockchain adoption through innovative solutions!
